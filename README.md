# AI-Energy-Demand-Forecasting
Hackathon project for AI-Based Energy Demand Forecasting for Smart Cities
## Project Overview

This project presents an AI-based system designed to forecast electricity demand in smart cities. 
By analyzing historical energy consumption, weather conditions, and real-time sensor data, the system predicts future energy demand and helps city administrators manage electricity distribution efficiently.

The goal is to reduce energy wastage, prevent power shortages, and support better integration of renewable energy sources.
## Problem Statement

Energy demand in smart cities fluctuates due to various factors such as weather conditions, population activities, and industrial usage. 
Traditional energy management systems often rely on historical averages, which may lead to inaccurate predictions.

This can result in power shortages, energy wastage, and inefficient electricity distribution.
## Proposed Solution

We propose an AI-powered energy demand forecasting system that uses machine learning models to analyze past energy consumption patterns and predict future electricity demand.

The system collects data from smart meters, IoT sensors, weather APIs, and historical databases. 
This data is processed and analyzed using machine learning algorithms to generate accurate demand forecasts.
## Key Features

- AI-based electricity demand forecasting
- Peak demand alert system
- Area-wise energy consumption prediction
- Renewable energy optimization
- Real-time monitoring dashboard
- Smart energy management recommendations
